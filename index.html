For the fourth station , the I Prefer This Colorful World Series of Hei Yiyang Exhibition Tour will be land in MUSEUM OF YOUNG ART on December 10 , 2016 at 3 pm. As part of the exhibition , Museum Of Young Art is presenting a series of learning programmes including a guests dialogue , children’s workshop—colorful plan（children art education public welfare） , opening ceremony, curators and artists led tours and Campus Lectures. We hope to encourage audiences to have a deeper engagement with and understanding of the exhibition , and to offer a broader context for discussion in the exploration of Chinese contemporary art.
The Guest Dialogue between Hei Yiyang and five experts from various circles —— “The Edge of Design and Art”. It is going to be held at 15:30 on Dec 10th , 2016 in Museum Of Young Art. Feng Yuan , Director of the School of Communication and Design at Sun Yat-Sen University , he is also the curator of this exhibition; Wu Xiaochuan , Graduate Director , Deputy Director of Discipline Construction office executive at Xi'an Academy of Fine Arts; Linghu Lei , Director of "LIFE" magazine and The Mix Place; Hei Yiyang , Founder and Host of XIANGDEMEI; Song Qun , Founder and Chief Editor of LOCAL；Huang Hongwei , Founder of ancient furniture brand , a Designer in architect and public space. They will discuss topics such as the edge of art and design , boundaries , media and innovation and practice and others , and look forward to the dialogue will bring the scene of the artists inspiration and ideological spark.
